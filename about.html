Web Designers' choice of colours

I have chosen the colour black along with the colour purple (#DB53FB) because I consider them as a welcoming combination in order to send a musical vibe, since this is the purpose of this web app. Furthermore, I do not prefer bright white color since it can be disturbing for some people to use it during the night. Consequently, since this is a music web app that can be used either at night or day, I have chosen the main color of the web app to be black because it's more relaxing to the eye.

In addition to that, I had to meet my client requirements as well. I achieved that by choosing the main color of the logo to be my client's choice. Moreover, I tone the words that I consider them more important than the rest with the same color. As a result, with the combination of a dark colour such as black in the backrounnd, the purple (#DB53FB) colour definitely stands out.
